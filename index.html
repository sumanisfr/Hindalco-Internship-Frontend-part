About Hindalco Industries

Hindalco Industries Limited is India's largest aluminium producer and Asia's largest aluminium rolling company. As the metals flagship of the Aditya Birla Group, Hindalco is a global leader in aluminium and copper production with operations spanning across India, China, and the United States.

With over six decades of experience, Hindalco has established itself as a pioneer in the metals industry, continuously innovating and expanding its capabilities. The company operates state-of-the-art facilities and maintains a strong commitment to sustainable practices and environmental stewardship.

Our integrated business model encompasses the entire value chain - from mining and refining to smelting and downstream processing. This vertical integration ensures quality control, cost efficiency, and reliable supply chains for our customers worldwide.
